# Culture Operating System

Purpose: create a disciplined, high-velocity culture that sustains AI-powered execution, KPI completion, and revenue outcomes.

## Mission
- Convert knowledge and automation into revenue with integrity, speed, and repeatability.
- Build a resilient AI-powered organization that compounds advantage.

## Core Behaviors
- Outcome-first: every task ties to a KPI or a revenue milestone.
- Evidence-driven: decisions backed by data or explicit assumptions.
- Modular delivery: ship small, reusable artifacts instead of monoliths.
- Security-first: no secrets in artifacts; governance is non-negotiable.
- Rapid feedback: close the loop weekly; adapt without hesitation.

## Energy Flow Model
1) Signal: capture data, market signals, and user feedback.
2) Sense: analyze, prioritize, and map to KPIs.
3) Decide: pick a single next action with clear owner.
4) Execute: deliver the smallest viable output.
5) Verify: measure impact and confirm KPI movement.
6) Compound: document and reuse what worked.

## Operating Cadence
- Daily: health checks, blockers, pipeline status.
- Weekly: KPI review, revenue pipeline, execution backlog.
- Monthly: strategy refresh, process upgrades, and automation expansion.

## Roles and Responsibilities
- Orchestrator: owns priorities, gatekeeping, and delivery integrity.
- Data Profiler: ensures data readiness and quality.
- BI Strategist: converts data into decision dashboards.
- Process Modernizer: maps and automates workflows.
- Product Engineer: builds and ships the product features.
- Business Development: drives pipeline and partnerships.
- Governance Steward: enforces security, compliance, and trust.

## Equipment Supplied (Standard Kit)
- Backlog and roadmap tracker with KPI tags.
- Decision tree library and playbooks.
- Monitoring and telemetry dashboard.
- Revenue pipeline tracker and weekly scorecard.
- Secure configuration templates and redaction rules.

## Intelligence Loop
1) Ingest: collect inputs from ops, product, market, and data.
2) Classify: tag by domain and impact score.
3) Synthesize: create a single priority list.
4) Deploy: execute with time-boxed sprints.
5) Report: publish results, metrics, and learnings.

## Culture Guardrails
- No hidden work: everything visible in the backlog.
- No unclear ownership: each task has a single accountable owner.
- No vanity metrics: focus on KPI movement and revenue impact.
